Vegan Pumpkin Pie

Description

A creamy and tasty Pumpkin pie made with pumpkin purée
Easy to make and easy to Bake.
Prep Time: 5 minutes
Total Time: 1 hr 5 minutes
Serving: 1 9" Pie

Ingredients

  • 1 1/3 cups Pumpkin purée

  • 3/4 cup Coconut Milk

  • 1/2 cup Brown Sugar

  • 1/4 cup Cornstarch

  • 1/4 cup Maple Syrup

  • 1 tspn Vanilla Extract

  • 2 tspn Pumpkin Pie Spice

  • 1/2 tspn Cinnamon

  • 1/2 tspn Salt

  • 1 9" Frozen Vegan Pie Crust

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350°F. Have unbaked 9" Pie crust ready.

  2. Add Pumpkin, coconut milk, brown sugar,cornstarch,maple syrup
    vanilla extract, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, and salt ro a large
    bowl. Mix well

  3. Pour the mixture into the pie crust and use a spatula to evenly
    spread mixture and smooth out the top.

  4. Bake for 60 minutes.Let cool and then chill in the fridge for 4 hours or
    overnight until it is completely set. Cut then serve!
